Why Spiralps?
Spiralps is a new Swiss based company developing and marketing natural nutraceutical / functional beverages, using algae as its core functional component. The first one is named "Spiralps - Natural Body Balance". Its main characteristic is that it's using "spiralina" algae which is a very healthy algae containing a lot of iron easy to digest by the body. Furthermore, it contains a lot of antioxidant, proteins, minerals, vitamins to provide a global and balanced effect on the body.
  Not just a mix of water, tea or some fruits (like many)
  100% natural with spiraling, herbs, fruits, water
  No additive sugar or E200…
  Never heated (to preserve intrinsic quality of its ingredients)
